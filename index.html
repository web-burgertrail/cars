FAQs

Frequently asked questions

What is Our Assured?
Our Assured is our quality certification program where every car is inspected at 200+ checkpoints, comes with a 1-year warranty, and is eligible for our 5-day return policy. Look for the teal badge on listings.
How do I book a test drive?
Click "Book Test Drive" on any car listing page. OurDrive delivers the car to your chosen location within a 2-hour window for a free test drive experience.
How long does car evaluation take?
Our certified inspectors typically complete the evaluation in 45–60 minutes. You'll receive a detailed report and best-price offer within 2 hours of the evaluation.
Can I get a loan to buy a car?
Yes! We partner with 20+ banks and NBFCs to offer competitive auto loans starting from 8.5% per annum. EMI starts as low as 5,999/month for select cars.
What documents do I need to sell?
You'll need RC (Registration Certificate), insurance papers, your Aadhaar/PAN, and bank account details for payment. Our team will guide you through the process step by step.
How fast will I receive payment?
Once you accept our offer and documents are verified, payment is transferred directly to your bank account within 24–48 hours. We guarantee the fastest payout in the industry.
